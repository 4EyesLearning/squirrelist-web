Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers to help you get started with SquirreList.

Can I try SquirreList Pro before purchasing?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial of SquirreList Pro so you can experience all the premium features before making a decision. No credit card is required to start your trial, and you can downgrade to the free plan anytime if you decide Pro isn't right for you.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your account settings. If you cancel, you'll continue to have access to Pro features until the end of your current billing period. After that, your account will automatically switch to the Free plan without losing any of your tasks.

Is there a limit to how many tasks I can create?

No, there's no limit to the number of tasks you can create in any of our plans. Even on the Free plan, you can create as many basic tasks as you need. The plan limitations are related to features like projects, collaborators, and advanced functions, not the quantity of tasks.

Is SquirreList really free to use?

Yes! You can use all core features for free. We also offer optional premium features for power users.

Can I use it offline?

Absolutely. Tasks added offline will automatically sync when you're back online.

Does it support syncing across devices?

Yes, your data is synced seamlessly across all your logged-in devices.

How do I back up my data?

Backups are automatic through cloud sync. You don't need to do anything manually.

Can I customize the look of the app?

Yes! SquirreList lets you choose themes, layout preferences, and dark/light modes.

What platforms does SquirreList support?

Currently available for iOS and Android. A web version is in our roadmap.
