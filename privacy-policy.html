Privacy Policy for SquirreList

Last Updated: Jan 21, 2026

Welcome to SquirreList, a playful to-do list app to help you "harvest your tasks" with Amrita the squirrel! This Privacy Policy explains how the 4EyesLearning Team ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). We are committed to safeguarding your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following data to provide and improve SquirreList:

  • Personal Information: Display name and email address (for account creation).
  • Usage Data: Tasks, task status (in progress, completed), streaks, and completion history.
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system (iOS/Android).
  • Notifications Data: Data needed to send reminders (if enabled).

We do not collect location, contacts, photos, or other sensitive information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide core features (task management, streaks, reminders).
  • Generate motivational messages (via Gemini API, if enabled).
  • Send push notifications for reminders.
  • Analyze usage to improve the App (via Firebase Analytics).

3. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your data. We may share it with:

  • Firebase (Google): For data storage, analytics, and notifications. See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Gemini API (Google): To generate motivational messages (if enabled). See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • AdMob (Google): Starting in version 2, for ads (opt-out available). See Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Children’s Privacy

SquirreList is not intended for children under 13. We restrict account creation for users under 13 to comply with COPPA. However, if a child uses a parent’s device, parents are responsible for supervising usage. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it immediately. Parents can contact us for assistance.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure Firebase servers, protected by Firebase Authentication and encryption. We take reasonable measures to safeguard your information, but no system is fully secure. Please notify us if you suspect a security breach.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access or update your personal information.
  • Delete your account and all associated data.
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection (e.g., analytics, ads in version 2).

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@4eyeslearning.com

7. Third-Party Links

Our App may link to third-party services (e.g., Google Privacy Policy). We are not responsible for their practices. Please review their policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Please review periodically.
